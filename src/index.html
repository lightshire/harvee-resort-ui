ABOUT US

Situated 1,500 feet above sea level at the foot of Mount Banahaw in the town of Lucban, Quezon. Batis Aramin Resort & Hotel brings you the comfort of home nestled in nature’s beauty. It is a 7-hectare magnificent rainforest garden.

A perfect destination for tourists seeking an escape from the usual hot weather in the country. 5-minute walk from the renowned Kamay ni Hesus Shrine, a sacred ground and pilgrimage site for everyone who wants to reflect, give thanks and pray for healing. Batis Aramin offers modern accommodation. Each and every room is designed and furnished with comfortable, customized amenities for a comfortable stay.

Enjoy an al fresco dining at the Pool Bar and indulge your taste buds with the savoury, authentic Lucban delicacies such as Pancit Hab-hab, Hardinera and Langgonisang Lucban.
